Instructions

Before you begin, let's practice. This will help you get used to the task. It also helps us determine if you are a proficient enough speaker of English to be qualified for this task.

Be careful. If you answer too many practice questions incorrectly, you will not be allowed to proceed with the task.

We are interested both in how well you understand the sentences, and how quickly you are able to answer. Take your time reading the sentences, but then, once the question has been displayed, answer as quickly and accurately as you can. However, accuracy is more important than speed, so don't rush.

Once the task begins you will not need to use your mouse. All input will be done using the keyboard. SPACE, ENTER, 1, and 2 are the only keys used. You may find it easiest to keep one hand near the number keys and the other near SPACE and ENTER.
